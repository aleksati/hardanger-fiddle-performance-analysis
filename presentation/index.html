Interactive Score

Through visually and interactive comparison. Gamification. Play game

We transform the Performance transcription into more friendly data-format. JSON dictionary. onset and duration ratios of every beat, note and bar

Basis for the all the tools.

virtue of ratios, enable making adjustments to the beats dynamically.

2

Inspecting melodic-rythmic structures (motifs)

Goal

  • Explore timing patterns from a motivic perspective.
  • Increase data accessibility through visualization

(Johansson, 2009, p.101)

Motifs are to be understood as reapting "melodic-rytmic" phrases, or sentences. usually after 2 measures in springar.

Further Development

  • Propegate beat adjusting to all motivic instances.
  • Dynamic plotting (MS instead of ratio percentage)
  • note scaling when adjusting beats.
